PTSD Protect the School Demo

The lights flicker, then fail. Silence fills the halls… for a moment. Then it begins. Something is coming for the school. Not once. Not twice. Over and over again — in waves that never truly stop. There’s no evacuation. No rescue. Whatever was supposed to protect this place is gone. All that’s left… is you. The classrooms stand empty, but they won’t stay untouched for long. Every corridor is a path. Every entrance, a weakness. If they get through, there is nothing left to slow them down. You must turn this fragile place into something it was never meant to be — a fortress. Build your defenses piece by piece. Place your towers carefully. Watch how they move, how they adapt, how they overwhelm. The waves will change. They will grow faster. Smarter. Less predictable. Some will rush past before you can react. Others will push straight through your strongest defenses. And some… will force you to rethink everything you thought would work. Resources are limited. Time is shorter. Every choice matters — and every mistake lingers. You are not meant to win easily. You are meant to endure. This is only the beginning. A fragment of something larger. A test to see how long you can hold on before the pressure breaks you. The school cannot defend itself. But it will fall… if you don’t.

Guide

Towers

Radar: reveals stealth in range so other towers can target them.

Bank: no attack, generates gold over time.

Tesla: chain damage plus Tesla Storm ability when you own 2+.

Launcher: burst damage and unlocks Orbital Barrage at 2+.

Tempest: creates pulse zones that control clustered enemies.

Enemies

Basic: balanced stats.

Fast: low HP, high speed.

Tank: high HP, very slow.

Armored: high armor, needs penetration.

Stealth Bomber: visible, but only targetable in Radar range.

Boss: appears every 5 waves.

Buttons

Start Wave: Starts the current wave.

Pause: Pauses the game.

Auto: Toggle for auto starting waves.

Speed: Toggle for game speed.

Special upgrades

Squad Link: +1 extra target per shot (max 3)

AP Shells: +4 armor penetration (max 3)

Deadeye: +1 pierce and faster reload (max 2)

Twin Barrels: +2 extra targets per shot (max 2)

Deep Freeze: stronger and longer slow (max 3)

Overcharge: +range and +damage pulse (max 3)

Shrapnel Core: +1 target and +2 armor penetration (max 3)

Ion Core: +damage and faster fire rate (max 2)

Maelstrom Core: more pulses and targets, slightly wider storm (max 2)

Compound Interest: +income per payout and faster payout rate (max 3)

Signal Amplifier: +radar range for stealth reveal (max 3)
