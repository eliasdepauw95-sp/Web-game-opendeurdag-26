Guide

Towers

Radar: reveals stealth in range so other towers can target them.

Bank: no attack, generates gold over time.

Tesla: chain damage plus Tesla Storm ability when you own 2+.

Launcher: burst damage and unlocks Orbital Barrage at 2+.

Tempest: creates pulse zones that control clustered enemies.

Enemies

Basic: balanced stats.

Fast: low HP, high speed.

Tank: high HP, very slow.

Armored: high armor, needs penetration.

Stealth Bomber: visible, but only targetable in Radar range.

Boss: appears every 5 waves.
